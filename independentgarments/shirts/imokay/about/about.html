“I’m okay” reversible tee


So, this tee is quite a mess which is exactly what makes me love it. It’s messy, it’s quite ugly, yet I think it’s quite perfect for what it’s trying to represent. This garment is meant to represent those times where I actually tell other people “I’m good”, or that “I’m fine”. When in reality my head is full of other things and most times, bad things.

On the outside of the garment (the right side) we have only two thoughts which are meant to represent what I tell people. On the inside of the garment (lining/wrong side) we have all those thoughts that can be subtly seen through the surface but not really put into words. The Right side represents the words I say. The wrong side represents the words I don’t.

It’s also the first garment that starts to use my branding. Which is a woven tag made out of a changing fabric (it’s meant to feel detached and like “it shouldn’t be there") with the fingerprint of my index finger.

also, the Woven Tag is stitched on the Wrong side of the fabric on purpose. The "True" way to use the Tee is to expose those bad thoughts. Which can be seen when using it "Correctly", but can't be read.
